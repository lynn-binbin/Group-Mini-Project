Gun Violence in Chicago : spatial and temporal analysis

Gun Violence

in Chicago

The criminal offences in USA metropolitans notably in Chicago in Oblivion state always emerge in the endless stream dating back to the last century. Although the intense situation of crime in Chicago has been slightly relieved due to different levels of gun control policy documents, the overall rate of gun violence in this ‘city of the devil’ has been higher than the average number in USA since 21st century. In 2016, Chicago itself unfortunately accounted for nearly a half of increase in homicides in the whole country. The percentage of crime with gun also shot up in 2016. Not only has gun violent crime grew rapidly over the past couple of years, but Chicago police increasingly fail to orient and then arrest the offenders. For details, just 17% of murders in Chicago were solved in 2017. Therefore, the Chicago’s general gun crime pattern now still remains unsolved. There exist mixed and complicated factors leading to such a serious situation. It involves racial conflicts in impoverished neighborhood, street gangs and public corruption. Additionally, one essential reason should not be ignored. when the gun control law in chicago becomes stricter, the federal judge or supreme court frequently think they are unconstitutional and then strike them down.

Policies

changing

1982

Overture

Chicago

In 1982, the mayor of Chicago, Jane Byrne, prohibited guns that weren’t already registered with Chicago police. But that ban was rejected by the Supreme Court in 2010.

2013

Firearm Concealed Carry Act

Illinois

In 2013, Illinois became the last state to enact the Firearm Concealed Carry Act, which established a system for the problem of lacking concealed carry licenses with clear requirements on how the gun owners apply for official licenses.

2014

Unconstitutional Law

Chicago

In 2014, since the old version law of banning the purchase of the firearms within city limits was considered to be unconstitutional by a federal judge, the city council of Chicago had to pass a new gun shop law, permitting gun stores while confining them to limited areas of the city, requiring that all gun sales must be clearly recorded with videos, and constraining buyers to one gun per 30-day period. Store owners must make their records accessible to the police.

2017

Appeal

Federal Appeals Court

A federal appeals court judged that the city's revised gun shop law was unconstitutional.

2018

New State Gun Law

Illinois

In 2018, New state gun law was published , amendments would take effect 1st January in 2019, Three amendments expand approaches to abolish a person’s FOID( Firearm Owners Identification )card and also prolong the gun-purchase waiting period of rifles to 72 hours, while a new law — the Firearms Restraining Order Act — builds up a process to make sure a person detach firearms from a person deemed dangerous to themselves or others.

Chicago

The data being used in this project was collected from the City of Chicago's open data portal. It is a website established by the city government of Chicago and provides all kind of official Chicago city data for free to every user. Many of these datasets were updated frequently, therefore, we were able to get the most recent official data of all reported incidents of crime that happened in the city of Chicago for the past ten years from 2009 to 2018. Our original data contains 10 datasets with a total of 3,100,319 rows of reported crime. With columns including ID, case number, date, block, IUCR code, primary type , description, location description, arrest, domestic, beat, district, ward, community area, FBI code, x coordinate and y coordinate, year, updated on, latitude and longitude, and location.

Unique Insight

CASA Crime Visualisation Group

The website would like to detailedly visualise the variation tendency of gun crime in Chicago from 2009 to 2018. It contains assault, battery, robbery, weapon violations and criminal sexual assault. Besides time analysis with scales of month and year to seek the main trend, the maps of spatial analysis with longitude and latitude aims to find out the distribution of gun crime. The website also attempts to provide users with the necessary reference by observing the historical data of the decade and predict the number of future shooting incidents in 2019-2021.
